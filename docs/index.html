Gameplay

You play as an Orc in this single-player platformer. After a knight destroys your village, it's up to you to avenge your people's death.

Fight through persistant grunts who are our hunting weak monsters. Prove to them that you are not just any foe. Your journey ends only when you kill the knight who crumbled your life.

With simple knockback combat, the orc wields a short spear and a throwing spear. Utilize both of these weapons as you traverse across pits, spikes, and other obstacles. Maybe your weapons can help you platform as well.

Characters

Orc

Evil monster or vengeful hero? Only the seeing can foretell. Nobody chooses to be born an orc.

Skillful in close melee and ranged combat, the orc wields spears. The orc's adaptability and toughness makes it a formitable species.

Grunt

Longing to wish a princess one day, he must survive the day, and that means purging the land of monsters.

Wielding a sword, the knight has medium ranged melee attacks. They hope killing monster will promote them into knighthood.

Knight

Thought as the heros of the land by many, does he show courage or arrogance?

Some of the best warriors in the land, the knight has many special attacks. He is confident and will ensure his attacks do not miss.
